# Global Economy

Archanid features a **Global Economy** system. This means your cash balance (`$`) follows you across every server that uses Archanid. This encourages users to be active in multiple communities to grow their wealth.

## Earning Cash

There are several ways to earn money:

### 1. Work
Get a job and earn a paycheck.
*   **Command:** `/economy work`
*   **Cooldown:** 1 Hour
*   **Reward:** Variable based on "Job" RNG.

### 2. Daily Reward
Claim your universal basic income.
*   **Command:** `/economy daily`
*   **Cooldown:** 24 Hours
*   **Reward:** Flat rate (e.g., $1,000).

### 3. Pray
Pray to the RNG gods. High risk, high reward.
*   **Command:** `/economy pray`
*   **Cooldown:** 5 Minutes
*   **Outcomes:**
    *   **Blessed:** Win huge cash ($1000+).
    *   **Ignored:** Nothing happens.
    *   **Cursed:** Lose a percentage of your wallet balance.

### 4. Trading
Send money to other users securely.
*   **Command:** `/economy give user:@friend amount:500`

---

## Leaderboards & Rank

See who rules the economy.

*   **Global Leaderboard:** `/economy leaderboard`
    *   Shows the Top 10 richest users across **all** servers.
*   **Personal Rank:** `/economy rank`
    *   Shows your Global Rank (e.g., #42), total cash, and inventory value.

---

## Inventory System

The economy includes collectible items.

*   **Check Inventory:** `/economy rank` (Shows item counts).
*   **Weed:** `/economy weed` - Check your stash.
*   **Beers:** `/economy beers` - Check your tab.

*(Note: Items are currently obtained through specific "Crime" or "Shop" events depending on server configuration.)*
