## Why Archanid?

### :material-drawing: Visual Architecture
Forget manually creating channels. Use the **Setup Wizard** to design your server's layout (Channels, Roles, Categories) in an interactive menu, then click "Build" to watch it happen instantly.

### :material-chart-line: Persistent Economy
Your wealth follows you. The currency you earn in one Archanid server is available in any other Archanid server, creating a massive, interconnected global market.

### :material-headphones: Studio Quality Music
Enjoy gapless playback, custom audio filters (Nightcore, Vaporwave, 8D), and seamless Spotify-to-YouTube bridging for the best listening experience.
